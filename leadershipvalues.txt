
Henrik L. Schou-Pedersen
My Leadership Values

Integrity
 Do:
 - what I say I will do
 - even when no-one is looking
 - hold myself to the same or higher standards as I expect of others
 Do not:
 - lie or cheat
 - promote only the principles which benefit me
 - brag or embellish the truth

Trust
 Do:
 - communicate clearly
 - state intentions and expectations
 - seek cooperation
 Do not:
 - pursue a hidden agenda
 - play politics, manipulation, favoritism, rivalry etc.
 - sabotage or counteract others behind their back

Reason
 Do:
 - focus on facts
 - be aware of psychological biases
 - question assumptions
 Do not:
 - act on instinct, prejudice, bias or impulse
 - bring personal views to work
 - go for the person in stead of the ball

Serenity
 Do:
 - stay calm
 - change what I can, accept what I cannot
 - use problems as opportunities
 Do not:
 - agitate trouble
 - speak in anger or resentment
 - complain about things that have happened or decisions already made

Kindness
 Do:
 - help others
 - accept differences and disagreements
 - assume others have good intentions
 Do not:
 - ridicule, belittle or attack others
 - hold grudges
 - argue for the sake of winning

Curiosity
 Do:
 - embrace change
 - question my assumptions
 - always keep learning
 Do not:
 - reject other perspectives
 - seek the one conclusive truth
 - defend the status quo

Creativity
 Do:
 - encourage ideas
 - encourage innovation
 - question established truth
 Do not:
 - reject ideas for being too abstract or impractical
 - seek conformity for its own sake
 - interpret and enforce rules rigidly
